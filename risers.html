RISER'S TOURNAMENT RULES & REGULATIONS

Eligibility: The tournament is open to all players residing anywhere, who are at least 13 years old and can play on the Mumbai server.

Registration: Teams must register online through the tournament website at least one week before the tournament start date. Each team must have five players, and each player can only play for one team. Solo players may form teams with other players in the ⁠risers-find-players channel.

Format: The tournament will be a single-elimination bracket for all matches, except for the semi-finals and finals, which will be best-of-three. The maximum number of teams will be 12. Maximum of Two Timeouts will be provided.

Game Settings: All games will be played on VALORANT's default competitive settings, with a maximum of 12 rounds per half.

Map Pool: The map pool for the tournament will be the standard custom map pool available in VALORANT, except Breeze & Icebox.

Server Location: All matches will be played on the Mumbai server only.

Game Rules: Any use of cheats, hacks, or exploits is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disqualification of the offending player and their team. Players are not allowed to use any coaching or receive outside assistance during the game.

Code of Conduct: All players are expected to behave in a sportsmanlike manner and treat each other with respect. Any verbal abuse, hate speech, or inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated and may result in disciplinary action.

Prize Pool: The prize pool for the tournament will be 2000 INR. Every participating team will receive a participation e-certificate.

Streaming: Matches from the semi-finals onwards will be streamed on YouTube.

Verification: The tournament organizers will verify the members of each team through the team leads.

Registration Deadline: January 19, 2024

Entry Fee: Rs 100 per team

Match Dates: Scheduled shortly after the registration closes. Kindly be patient.

Changes to Rules: The tournament organizer reserves the right to modify the rules at any time for any reason. Any changes to the rules will be communicated to the participating teams as soon as possible.
